Rock Paper Scissors

Welcome to the game!

This is a classic game of rock, paper, scissors. The rules are simple:

  1. Click "Start" to begin playing.
  2. Select one of the three options: Rock, Paper or Scissors.
  3. The computer will then randomly pick one as well.
  4. A scoreboard on the screen will keep track of your wins, losses and draws.
  5. Remember: Scissors > Paper, Paper > Rock, Rock > Scissors.
  6. First to 5 wins!
